GOALS

The game takes place in a virtual world where players are divided into three camps: Deer, Bears, and Hunters.

The goal of the Deer is to survive and avoid being hunted and killed by Bears and Hunters.

Bears attempt to hunt and kill Deer while avoiding being killed by Hunters.

Hunters hunt Deer and Bears while being wary of other Hunters.

PROGRESSION

Each player starts as a level 1 Deer.

To progress, Deer must avoid being successfully hunted until reaching level 10.

Once becoming a level 10 Bear, the player can hunt and kill Deer while avoiding being killed by Hunters.

Bears can progress to become level 10 Hunters by hunting and killing Deer and Bears.

MECHANICS

To initiate a hunt as a Bear or Hunter, go to the hunting map and identify your prey.

When you are close enough, the "Hunt Complete" button will light up, allowing you to confirm your kill.

Deer receive warnings when Bears or Hunters approach, giving them a chance to escape or hide.

Players must cooperate and compete to survive and progress in the game.
